What Code Wod is about

The purpose:
This is a workout designed with first responders in mind. Behind cancer, Heart Attack is the leading killer of first responders. We are the only ones who can prevent it. We work in shorter bursts of intense labor. Usually, we're at 100% capacity for 5 to 20 minutes. Look at it as a full scba bottle used, or a foot chase. These workouts are designed to train within that time domain.

None of the workouts posted will be flashy, technical, or overly heavy. If done with intensity and purpose, you will get healthier.

The goal:
We're not here to be the strongest, we're here to go home. and preferable with no injury. I feel strongly this has been of benifit to my department and I hope more people can benifit as well.

“The goal is to get fit, make it the best hour of your day, stay safe, turn up the music, high five some people, and blow off some steam. So remember that. Relax. Have fun. Work out.”
